Property Features

  • Natural Springs:
    Our property is blessed with numerous natural springs, providing a continuous flow of fresh, pristine water sourced directly from the heart of the Ozarks. While the water from these springs is pure and untouched by human pollution, we recommend using caution and filtering it before consumption, especially during certain times of the year when natural fluctuations may occur. Enjoy the beauty and tranquility of these natural springs as they nourish the surrounding landscape and create a serene oasis for relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Meandering Creek:
    A tranquil creek meanders its way through the property, offering the perfect setting for leisurely walks, peaceful picnics, and refreshing dips on hot summer days. The gentle babbling of the creek and the lush vegetation lining its banks create a serene atmosphere for relaxation and exploration.
  • Caves to Explore:
    Delve into the fascinating world of our property's caves, each one holding its own mysteries and wonders waiting to be discovered. From small, intimate chambers to expansive caverns adorned with stunning rock formations, these caves offer endless opportunities for adventurous spelunkers to marvel at the natural beauty hidden beneath the surface.
  • Scenic Waterfalls:
    Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of our property's scenic waterfalls as they cascade gracefully down rugged cliffs into shimmering pools below. Whether you're seeking a picturesque backdrop for photography or a refreshing spot to cool off after a hike, these waterfalls promise breathtaking views and unforgettable moments in nature.
  • Diverse Wildlife:
    Immerse yourself in the rich biodiversity of our property, home to a diverse array of wildlife species both big and small. From majestic deer and playful squirrels to colorful songbirds and elusive woodland creatures, the natural habitat surrounding our property provides endless opportunities for wildlife enthusiasts to observe and appreciate the wonders of nature.
  • Community Hub:
    In the center of the property is a concrete circle where we envision building a vibrant community hub. This hub will serve as the focal point of our village, featuring a large circular room akin to a giant gazebo. It will boast a stage for live music, a fire pit for gathering, a communal kitchen, and restrooms. While the community hub is still in the planning phase, our existing cabin serves as the temporary gathering spot until its completion.
